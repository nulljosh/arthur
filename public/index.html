About

How It Works

The pipeline starts with data preparation and training in PyTorch, then serializes model artifacts for runtime targets. Inputs are tokenized to character indices, transformed through attention blocks, and decoded into next-token probabilities for fast inference in C99 or ONNX.

training -> tokenize -> transform -> infer
